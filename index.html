OUR STORY

We are a family run and owned business with humble beginnings. Emphasis was first established in the latter months of 1999 by Aristotle & Vicky's passion to continue the family tradition from Greece. Coming from the fastidious European market, we have continued this ancient craft for 24 years in Australia, with prior bricks and mortar locations at Toowoong Village and Wynnum Plaza in Brisbane, Australia. We now operate out of our Studio Workshop in the quiet suburb of Tingalpa, servicing customers both national and international.
